05 · Operator Dilemma
Trend is not strategy.

06 · The Trend Filter
Every trend must
earn its role.

Frequency

Does it bring members back more often?

Retention

Does it make the relationship harder to leave?

Community

Does it create shared identity and belonging?

LTV

Does it expand monetization without damaging trust?

Scalability

Can it be trained, operated, and repeated across clubs?

Ecosystem Depth

Does it connect to other products and outcomes?

06
07 · Not All Trends Serve The Same Role
Different trends.
Different jobs.

Strength

The new mainstream core: confidence, capability, functional ageing.

Pilates

Premium retention through technique, progression, and coaching.

HYROX

Community, identity, events, and training purpose.

Padel / Pickle

Social acquisition and lifestyle frequency beyond the gym floor.

Recovery

Lifetime value through readiness, consistency, and longevity.

PT

Monetization depth through trust, accountability, and personalization.
